4.5. GENERATE TECH PACK
Tech Pack, PDF output of the specification components will be generated by Client XYZ team for
requesting samples or initiating production.

2. Select Specification from the Drop down. Source is automatically selected.
3. Scroll down to Specification section and click on
icon to generate Tech Pack.
