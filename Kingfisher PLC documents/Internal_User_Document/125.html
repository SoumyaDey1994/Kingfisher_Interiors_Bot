5.2 GROUPING IN SEARCH RESULTS
Client XYZ users can get an overview of all Colorforms under the Library for a GBA, Season,
Colorform Category etc. by the below illustrated steps
1. Go to the search Results.
2. Click on the attribute to be grouped (Example -GBA).
3. Search Results will be grouped by Attribute value (Example- GBA is grouped into Eyes Wide open and
Love Mondays here.
