5.4 EXPORT COLORFORM TO PDF AND EXCEL FORMATS
Client XYZ and CLIENT XYZ sourcing teams can export Colorform from the Library into PDF and excel
formats to share with vendors or users outside the FlexPLM system.
1. Go to Libraries
2. Click on Colorform.
3. Enter the Criteria for Search.
(Example- Here, We have selected the Colorform view and Filter for 2020 SS EMEA filter to get the
relevant Search Results. All the Colorforms for the season 2020 SS EMEA will be displayed as shown
below.)
4. Click on the
icon to get the export of Search results in the form of an Excel sheet and
icon
to get the PDF export.
