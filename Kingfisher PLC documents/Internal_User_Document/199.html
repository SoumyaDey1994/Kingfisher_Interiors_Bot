7.5 COLORWAY LEVEL COSTING
Colorway Level Costing enables to capture Costing Specific to a colorway. Cost Sheet specific to a
Product colorway can be created.
Note:
For Colorway Level costing to take place, Colorway variation BOM should be associated in order to
BOM Roll up to happen.
1. Navigate to
Product > Sourcing > Costing
.
2. Select
Create Cost Sheet
option in Action dropdown list under Cost Sheet list tab.
